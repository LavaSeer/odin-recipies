Crab Rangoon Recipie

YOU LIKE RAGOONS?! I LOVE RAGOONS!! I CAN'T GET ENOUGH OF EM! ITS A DEEP FRIED COUPLING OF SUPPLE CRAB MEAT! YA CANT SAY NO TO THAT!

Ingredients

  • a whole crab
  • mayonnaise
  • a whole rangoon

Instructions

  1. cut the crab and min in mayonnaise
  2. add a small portion of the crab mayo mix into each rangoon
  3. poof! crab rangoons!
