Salad

A delicacy of the meatless! chock full of vegetables, croutons, and dressings!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of lettuce
  • 1/2 cup of croutons
  • 1 tomato
  • dressing of your choice

Instructions

  1. dice the tomato
  2. mix in the diced tomato with the lettuce and croutons
  3. pour the dressing into the mix
